DIAMOND CUT: GOOD

Good Cut diamonds are cut using a technique which attempts to create the largest carat size while reflecting most of the light that enters it. A Good Cut stone will have slightly less fire than a Very Good or Premium but will still sparkle with a level of brilliance that is appropriate for most jewelry.

DIAMOND COLOR: E

E color stones are absolutely colorless to the naked eye and only a trained gemologist can distinguish between a D and E color stone by using special machinery. E color stones are very rare and are considered top of the line.

DIAMOND CLARITY: VS1 (FEW INCLUSIONS AT 10X)

VSI clarity diamonds have a very few, difficult to see inclusions. The minor inclusions are very difficult to see under 10x magnification and close to invisible to the naked eye. VS1 diamonds present a very good value opportunity because they are considered extremely clean stones and are considerably less expensive than the higher clarity grades.
